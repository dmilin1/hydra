# Downloading Media

You can download images and videos directly to your device's photo library through the share sheet or context menu.

## Saving a Video

**Long press** the video, select **"Share"** from the context menu, then tap **"Save Video"** in the share sheet. The video will be downloaded and saved to your photo library.

## Saving an Image

**Long press** the image and select **"Save Image"** to save it directly to your photo library. Alternatively, select **"Share"** and then tap **"Save Image"** in the share sheet.

## Permissions

Make sure Hydra has permission to access your photo library. If prompted, grant access so media can be saved. You can manage this in your device's Settings under Privacy & Security > Photos.
